This is Nailong!

Nailong (often called the "Milk Dragon") is a chubby, yellow cartoon dinosaur from a Chinese 3D animated series known for his round belly and goofy expressions. He has become a popular internet meme and digital mascot, beloved for his mischievous personality and viral dance clips.
